TITLE: Data Scientist
LOCATION: Bangalore, Karnataka, India
REPORTS TO: Head of Analytics
ROLE OVERVIEW
Work with cross-functional teams to derive insights from data, build predictive models, and deliver dashboards. This role suits early career professionals passionate about data.

RESPONSIBILITIES
• Analyze structured and unstructured data to uncover trends.
• Build and validate regression and classification models.
• Communicate findings to business stakeholders.
• Develop dashboards in Tableau/Power BI.
• Support data collection and ETL processes.

REQUIREMENTS
• B.Tech / MSc / MS in CS, Statistics, Mathematics or related.
• 1–3 years experience in data analysis or modeling.
• Strong SQL and Python skills.
• Familiarity with ML fundamentals.
• Good communication skills.
